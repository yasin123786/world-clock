Hour Hand
--------------
12 hour	= 360 deg
1 hour	= 30 deg

60 min	= 30 deg
1 min	= 1/2 deg
m min	= m/2 deg

Hour hand movement from 12 = (30 * hours + min/2) deg
--------------------------------------------------------
Minute Hand
--------------
60 min	= 360 deg
1 min	= 6 deg

Minute hand Movement from 12 = (6 * min ) deg
--------------------------------------------------------
Seconds Hand
--------------
60 second	= 360 deg
1 second	= 6 deg
Seconds hand Movement from 12 = (6 * sec ) deg
